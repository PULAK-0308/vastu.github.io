Shri Vinayak Vastu

Factory Vastu

It is essential to apply Vastu in any type of industrial plant. And Vastu should also be implemented in the industrial sector. It is often seen that a person is engaged in improving the Vastu of his house, he does not pay attention to Vastu in his office, business area, industrial area etc.

At the time of construction of any type of factory, if we build the factory keeping in mind the five elements, then even in this difficult and economic scenario, we can achieve all the goals with the use of Vastu, which is set in our destiny, in which obstruction is coming due to Vastu defects.

Even in the factory, we should construct the plot of the factory keeping in mind the size of the factory plot, the main entrance of the factory, the place of manufacture of goods in the factory, the place of sale of goods after manufacture, etc. according to Vastu. Things to keep in mind while building Location of owner's office, location of temple, location for machinery, location of both heavy machine and light machine separately, electrical equipment (electric meter, generator etc.) location of training of new workers, location of toilet etc.

If we ignore all the things then we may have to go through the following problems like:- Stealing of goods by labor in the field or otherwise, strikes and demands by trade union, sudden accidents, low productivity, sudden breakdown of machines. going bad, falling sales, bankruptcy, heavy debt, etc.
