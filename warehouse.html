Shri Vinayak Vastu

Warehouse Vastu

If you want to make your warehouse according to Vastu Shastra then it is very good.The first thing you need to do is to make sure that your warehouse is large enough to accommodate all of your inventory and storage needs. You also need to think about how much space will be needed for each type of storage system or equipment once it is in place. Once you determine how much space you will need for each type of item, then think about their placement within that warehouse space as per Vastu.
Important points to be noted for godown as per Vastu Shastra:-

1. First of all selection of size of warehouse premises.
2. The godown should be selected after paying attention to the roads, ditches, pits, rivers, drains, mountains etc. around the godown premises.
3. Special attention should be paid to the main entrance and exit doors in the godown.
4. Parking area of the godown.
5. Place for weighing goods in godown as per direction.
6. According to the direction, on the stairs to be built in the godown and the place of the lift.
7. Location of heavy goods to be kept in godown.
8. Location of liquid goods to be kept in the godown.
9. Location of the Pantry of the godown.
10. Location of underground water tank in godown.
11. Location of electric meter, generator, solar power plant etc. to be installed in the godown according to direction.
12. Proper location of overhead tank in godown as per direction.
13. Slope of the roof of the godown, special attention should be paid to the thickness of the wall on all four sides.
14. Getting the basement constructed in the godown as per the direction.
15. Construction of ladies and gents toilets in the godown as per direction , etc.
