Shri Vinayak Vastu

Shop Vastu

Before starting any kind of shop, you should start by showing the Vastu of that shop completely from your Vastu consultant.
Things to be noted in the Vastu of the shop are:-

shop entrance
position of salesman sitting in the shop
shop peon seat
temple site
Place for placing incense sticks in the temple
safe deposit box
cash box storage
a place to keep goods that have not been sold for a long time so that they can be sold quickly

Note: – The entrance of the shop is determined according to the planets related to the goods kept in the shop.

To get complete information related to this, contact the consultant of Shri Vinayak Vastu.
